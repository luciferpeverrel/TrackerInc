About Us

An innovation generated out of curiosity which can turn the tables for the stolen and lost valuables in your bags.

Incorporated as the first Minor project for our Under graduate studies, these are the few of Requirements which is deemed necessary for everyone's felicity

  • The product should be able to track the baggage when placed inside the bag
  • The product should be able to notify the given location in latitude and longitude
  • The product should display the location on the Ide terminal
  • The project should be able to send text message of the location to the number provided
